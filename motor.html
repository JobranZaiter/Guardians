Motor Insurance

General Overview

Motor insurance is a policy designed to protect you and other motorists on the road. In case of an unforeseen accident or theft, you would benefit from coverage for financial losses and damage done to your vehicle, including protection against potential medical expenses and damages caused to a third party during the collision. In short, by taking the appropriate measure to safeguard your costly car investment, you would finally gain some well-deserved peace of mind.

Motor Insurance Coverages

Third-Party Liability Bodily Injuries (Compulsory)

If you own a private car, this policy provides you with compensation for bodily injuries that you may cause to a third party in the case of a covered accident. Limit - up to 500,000 USD.

Total Loss

Replacing a damaged vehicle with a new equivalent is sometimes more cost-effective. The car is declared a total loss when its estimated repair expenses exceed 65% of the vehicle's value.

Third-Party Liability Material Damages

As a private car owner, The TPL policy provides compensation for material damages you may cause to a third party when you meet with a covered accident.

All Risks

It is the policy that covers your private car from any risk. The compensation you get includes your vehicle's covered registration fees, protection against fires, theft, damages, and material damage your insured car may cause to any third party. When you choose this policy, you basically protect your finances, health, and overall life.
